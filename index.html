ABOUT

Helping Hands is an unified platform where users publish help offers and requests. Its main objective is to simplify volunteering work so that anyone can jump right in.

The idea behind Helping Hands came from the overwhelming feeling some people suffer when trying to find volunteering opportunities.

Of course, every service on the platform is 100% free. Our main focus are those in need and anyone willing to help. The only costs users need to account for are transportation or any material needed for the specific help offer or request.
